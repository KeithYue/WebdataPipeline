									<h1 class="entry-title">对美韩实施“双反” 中国多晶硅业获喘息机会</h1>
										<div class="comments-link">
					<a href="http://blog.china5e.com/caoxi/archives/8601#respond" title="《对美韩实施“双反” 中国多晶硅业获喘息机会》上的评论"><span class="leave-reply">发表回复</span></a>				</div><!-- .comments-link -->

				<div class="entry-content">
			<p><P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">7月18日，媒体报道称，我国已对美国多晶硅产品的“反倾销、反补贴”诉讼正式立案，对韩国多晶硅产品的“反倾销”发起调查。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">或许正是受此消息提振，截止（7月18日）收盘，A股多晶硅板块呈现普涨态势。超日太阳、拓日新能、天威保变、川投能源、江苏阳光等个股涨幅均超1%。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">不完全统计数据显示，受美、韩等向我国低价“倾销”多晶硅的影响，自2011年9月以来，我国80%多晶硅企业已被迫停产，殃及全行业超过500亿元投资，造成失业人数超过5000人。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">接受《证券日报》记者采访的中国有色金属工业协会硅业分会相关人士表示，“如果最终管理层对美韩征收多晶硅‘双反’关税，那将为我国多晶硅行业争取到一些发展的时间。但想要从根本上扭转这一局面，还有赖于国内企业自身技术水平的提高”。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><STRONG>激增的多晶硅进口量</STRONG></P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">事实上，中、美、韩三国间在多晶硅贸易上的争执由来已久。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">早在2011年年底，中国有色金属工业协会硅业分会就曾公开提出过“对美韩等多晶硅出口国征收反倾销关税”的建议。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">彼时，硅业分会的理由是“多晶硅价格自2011年5月开始持续回落。截至11月底，已较5月均价下跌70%。但是在多晶硅价格下跌同时，国外多晶硅市场冲击却不断变强，导致国内进口量不断增加”，硅业分会会长赵家生曾在《近期国内硅产业变化以及建议》的报告中指出。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">最终，2011年我国总计进口多晶硅6.46万吨，同比增长36%，涉及进口金额25.9亿美元。其中，自美、韩两国进口的多晶硅占比约6成。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">今年1月，为减缓大量进口对我国多晶硅业造成的冲击，洛阳中硅、江苏中能、陕西天宏、赛维LDK等4家企业正式向商务部提交申请，要求对美国产多晶硅进行反倾销、反补贴调查。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">然而，一边是我国80%多晶硅企业被迫停产，另一边则是全球多晶硅业出现了产能过剩的情况。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">长江证券研报指出，与&nbsp;2012&nbsp;年全球光伏装机容量相比，全球前4大多晶硅生</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">产巨头——GCL-Poly、OCI、Hemlock、</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">Wacker&nbsp;合计产能基本可以满足全球全年多晶硅需求，多晶硅产能过剩情况较为严重。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">但就是在全球多晶硅行业产能过剩以及多晶硅价格朝着生产成本不断逼近的背景下，此前，韩国最大的多晶硅生产商OCI还提出了扩产计划，只不过，这一计划最终被OCI暂停了。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><STRONG>反败为胜依靠技术提升</STRONG></P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">当然，大量进口多晶硅并不是“我国80%多晶硅企业被迫停产”的首要原因。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">长江证券研报认为，多晶硅行业的危机与“欧债危机、欧洲传统光伏装机大国大幅下调政策补贴”具有直接关系。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">不过在面临同样的恶略环境时，多晶硅生产“龙头”却可以依靠规模和技术上的优势，在有效降低成本的同时维持一定水平的毛利率。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">以全球第一大多晶硅生产企业保利协鑫为例，其2012&nbsp;年第一季度多晶硅产量12335公吨，平均成本为19.7美元/公斤；对外销售3041公吨，平均售价为25.98美元/公斤，毛利率为24%左右。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">“一些国际巨头充分利用规模和技术的优势，再配合经济及光伏业恶略的大环境，在多晶硅行业恶性竞争”，一位不愿具名的业界人士向《证券日报》记者坦言。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">上述硅业分会相关人士向《证券日报》记者还表示，“在多晶硅价格大幅下滑的过程中，一些国际多晶硅企业不惜微利，甚至赔本向中国市场倾销多晶硅”。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">“从战略的角度出发，中国不能让他们抢夺光伏，乃至整个新能源市场的控制权和制高点。如果最终管理层对美韩征收多晶硅‘双反’关税，那将为我国多晶硅行业争取到一些发展的时间”，该硅业分会相关人士认为。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">不过必须认识到的是，我国多晶硅业想要真正的反败为胜还需依靠自身的技术水平提升。据介绍，仅从价格角度来看，我国多晶硅价格普遍在30美元/公斤以上，而进口多晶硅则在25美元/公斤以下。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; BORDER-RIGHT-WIDTH: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 2em; MARGIN: 0px 0px 1em; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; BORDER-TOP-WIDTH: 0px; BORDER-BOTTOM-WIDTH: 0px; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(51,51,51); BORDER-LEFT-WIDTH: 0px; WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; border-image: initial; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><SPAN style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; BACKGROUND-COLOR: rgb(255,255,255); TEXT-INDENT: 0px; DISPLAY: inline !important; FONT: 12px/26px simsun, Tahoma, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; FLOAT: none; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(169,169,169); WORD-SPACING: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">来源：证券日报</SPAN></P></p>
