									<h1 class="entry-title">发改委上调成品油或为新定价机制预热</h1>
										<div class="comments-link">
					<a href="http://blog.china5e.com/caoxi/archives/7558#respond" title="《发改委上调成品油或为新定价机制预热》上的评论"><span class="leave-reply">发表回复</span></a>				</div><!-- .comments-link -->
					</header><!-- .entry-header -->

				<div class="entry-content">
			<p><P><SPAN style="WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; DISPLAY: inline !important; FONT: 14px/25px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; FLOAT: none; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 2月8日零时，</SPAN>发改委<SPAN style="WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; DISPLAY: inline !important; FONT: 14px/25px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; FLOAT: none; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">上调汽柴油零售价，涨幅均300元/吨。约合柴油上调0.26元/升，汽油上调0.22元/升。调整后国内汽柴油最高零售价均价分别为9380元/吨和8530元/吨，分别上调3.30%和3.65%。业内分析人士表示，此次发改委及时提前调价或是为新定价机制推出预热。</SPAN></P><SPAN style="WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; DISPLAY: inline !important; FONT: 14px/25px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; FLOAT: none; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; LIST-STYLE-TYPE: none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; MARGIN: 12px 0px; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/25px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　据卓创资讯监测数据， 到目前三地变化率是3.81%，三地油价变化率已到达+4%调价临界点，市场上调价预期强烈。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; LIST-STYLE-TYPE: none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; MARGIN: 12px 0px; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/25px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　值得注意的是，此次的及时调价被业内人士认为是成品油定价机制改革推出的先导。自2011年10月9日发改委调价以来，成品油定价机制改革的推出成为业界关注的重点。此前，有消息人士在接受本报记者的采访时表示，2012年成品油定价机制改革推出的可能性加大。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; LIST-STYLE-TYPE: none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; MARGIN: 12px 0px; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/25px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　卓创认为，此次调价或是遵循新的机制而来。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; LIST-STYLE-TYPE: none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; MARGIN: 12px 0px; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/25px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　距离2011年10月9日调价后的第84个工作日，国内汽柴油价格迎来开年首次上调。自去年10月9日上调价格之后，国内柴油资源持续紧张，国内大部分地区柴油价格面临批零倒挂局面。卓创资讯研究员表示，但伴随着此次价格上调，国内此种局面将被打破。</P><br />
<P style="PADDING-BOTTOM: 0px; WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; LIST-STYLE-TYPE: none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; MARGIN: 12px 0px; PADDING-LEFT: 0px; PADDING-RIGHT: 0px; FONT: 14px/25px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; PADDING-TOP: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><SPAN style="WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; FONT: 12px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(134,134,134); WORD-SPACING: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px" class=gray>来源：</SPAN><A style="WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; FONT: 12px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; TEXT-DECORATION: none; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px" href="http://zqrb.ccstock.cn/" target=_blank>证券日报</A><SPAN style="WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; DISPLAY: inline !important; FONT: 12px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; FLOAT: none; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">&nbsp;<SPAN class=Apple-converted-space>&nbsp;</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; FONT: 12px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(134,134,134); WORD-SPACING: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px" class=gray>作者：</SPAN><FONT style="WIDOWS: 2; TEXT-TRANSFORM. none; TEXT-INDENT: 0px; FONT: 12px 宋体; WHITE-SPACE: normal; ORPHANS: 2; LETTER-SPACING: normal; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); WORD-SPACING: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px" color=#000000>夏青</FONT></P></SPAN></p>
